How Champion Rush Works ▾
Point Rush: Top 6 teams from the previous stage receive headstart carryover points (1st→10, 2nd→7, 3rd→5, 4th→3, 5th→2, 6th→1) that carry into the Grand Finals.
Champion Rush: Once a team crosses the point threshold (default: 80), they become ★ eligible. An eligible team must then win a Booyah (1st place) in a subsequent match to immediately close out the tournament and be crowned champion.
Scoring: Total Points = Placement Points + (Kills × Points Per Kill) + Headstart Carryover.
